Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Cric Info ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using the Cric Info mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and identity information: name, phone number, email address, role, and profile details you provide.
  • Authentication data: OTP verification data for sign-in and account recovery.
  • Booking and business data: grounds/academies, bookings, schedules, batches/students, fees, attendance, announcements.
  • Payment and payout details: bank account information submitted for owner payout setup.
  • Media and files: images/files you upload, such as profile photos, ground images, ownership proof, and attachments.
  • Device and technical data: diagnostics, metadata, and crash/performance information to improve reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage accounts.
  • Authenticate login and protect account security.
  • Provide booking, academy, and communication features.
  • Support payout and operations workflows.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • Improve service performance and prevent abuse/fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only when required with:

  • Infrastructure and hosting providers.
  • Communication providers (for OTP and notifications).
  • Payment/banking partners for payout workflows.
  • Legal/regulatory authorities where required by law.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract to provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests for security, reliability, and abuse prevention.
  • Legal obligations under applicable law.
  • Consent where required.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for operations, legal compliance, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and enforcement of agreements. Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No internet transmission or storage method is fully secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact: support@groundwale.com

You may also request to know categories of data collected/shared, request rectification, request portability, and where applicable opt out of sale or sharing by emailing: support@groundwale.com with subject "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information".

8. Region-Specific Rights

  • GDPR (EU/UK): access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of sale/sharing where applicable.
  • LGPD (Brazil): confirmation, access, correction, anonymization, portability, and deletion rights.
  • VCDPA (Virginia): access, correction, deletion, portability, and opt-out rights for targeted advertising/sale/profiling where applicable.

9. Children

Cric Info is not intended for children under 13 without verified parent/guardian and organization supervision.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will revise the effective date above.
