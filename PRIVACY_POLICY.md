## 2. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

- User account information: name, phone number, email address, role, and profile information.
- Authentication data: OTP verification data and login events.
- Booking and operations data: ground and academy details, bookings, schedules, attendance, fee records, announcements, and support interactions.
- Payment and payout data: bank account and payout configuration details submitted by account owners.
- User content and uploads: photos, images, attachments, and proof documents uploaded by users.
- Device and technical identifiers: IP address, device identifiers, app diagnostics, and service logs.
- Potentially sensitive data only when provided by users or required for service workflows and legal compliance.

## 3. Why We Process Personal Data

We process personal data to:

- provide account creation, authentication, and access control,
- deliver booking, scheduling, academy, communication, and support features,
- process payments and payouts,
- detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or abuse,
- maintain security, reliability, and performance,
- comply with legal and regulatory obligations,
- enforce contractual terms and resolve disputes.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR and similar regimes)

Where applicable, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

- Contract: processing needed to provide the services you request.
- Legitimate interests: service improvement, security, abuse prevention, and operational analytics.
- Legal obligation: compliance with legal, accounting, tax, and regulatory duties.
- Consent: where legally required (for example specific communications or optional features).

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell personal data for money.

We may share personal data with:

- cloud hosting and infrastructure providers,
- OTP, communications, and notification service providers,
- payment and banking partners for payout-related operations,
- analytics, logging, and support tooling providers,
- regulators, law enforcement, courts, or government bodies when required by law,
- professional advisers and auditors under confidentiality obligations.

If sharing could be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under some laws, users can opt out as described in Section 8.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as reasonably necessary for service delivery, account administration, legal compliance, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and contract enforcement.

Typical retention criteria:

- active account data: retained while account is active,
- transactional records: retained as required by law and financial regulations,
- support and security logs: retained for operational and security windows,
- deletion requests: fulfilled within applicable legal timelines unless retention is legally required.

## 7. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data, including access controls, encryption in transit where supported, and monitoring. No system is completely secure.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- access personal data we hold about you,
- correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data (rectification),
- request deletion of personal data,
- know categories of personal data collected, used, shared, sold, or disclosed,
- receive a portable copy of certain personal data,
- object to or restrict certain processing,
- withdraw consent where processing is based on consent,
- opt out of sale/sharing/targeted advertising where applicable,
- non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

How to exercise rights:

- Email support@groundwale.com with subject "Privacy Request"
- Include your account identifier and country/state
- We may verify your identity before processing requests

How to opt out of sale/sharing/targeted advertising (where applicable):

- Send "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" request to support@groundwale.com

## 9. Region-Specific Notices

### GDPR (European Economic Area and UK)

Users have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and withdrawal of consent. Users also have the right to lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority.

### CCPA/CPRA (California)

California users may request to know, delete, and correct personal information, and may opt out of sale/sharing where applicable.

### LGPD (Brazil)

Brazil users may request confirmation of processing, access, correction, anonymization, portability, deletion, and information about sharing.

### VCDPA (Virginia)

Virginia users may access, correct, delete, and obtain data portability, and may opt out of targeted advertising, sale, and profiling where applicable.

## 10. Children

Cric Info is not intended for children under 13 without verified parent or guardian and organizational supervision where applicable. If we learn we collected data unlawfully from a child, we will take steps to delete it.

## 11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards under applicable laws.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates with a revised effective date.
